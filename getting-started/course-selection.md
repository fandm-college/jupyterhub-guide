# Selecting a Course

## At a glance

- Select the course you would like to access.
- Click **Start**.
- Wait while your course starts.
- JupyterLab opens automatically.

If you are associated with more than one course, you will see the **Course Selection** page after logging in.

To access a course:

1. Select the course you would like to access from the **Course** drop-down menu.
2. Click **Start**.

{: .expect }
> After clicking **Start**, your course may take a few seconds to launch. You may briefly see a message indicating that your course is starting. When the course is ready, JupyterLab opens automatically.

{: .good-to-know }
> You may notice the word **spawning** during this process. This is JupyterHub's term for preparing your course environment and is a normal part of starting a course.

If you teach only one course, you will skip the Course Selection page and be taken directly to your course after logging in.
