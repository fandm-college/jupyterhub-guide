# Logging In

## At a glance

- Go to **https://jupyterhub.fandm.edu**.
- Sign in with your Franklin & Marshall NetID and password.
- Select a course if prompted.

Open your browser and navigate to:

[https://jupyterhub.fandm.edu](https://jupyterhub.fandm.edu)

Sign in using your Franklin & Marshall **NetID** and password.

![F&M JupyterHub login screen]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/screenshots/jupyterhub-login.png){: .figure }

<div class="figure-caption">
<strong>Figure 2.</strong> Log in to F&M JupyterHub using your Franklin & Marshall NetID and password.
</div>

{: .expect }
> After logging in, one of two things will happen. If you are associated with only one course, JupyterHub will take you directly to that course. If you are associated with multiple courses, you will be asked to choose which course you want to access.
